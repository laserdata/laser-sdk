# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include showing empathy and kindness, respecting differing opinions and experiences, giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully, taking responsibility for mistakes, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or imagery, trolling or insulting comments, personal or political attacks, public or private harassment, publishing others' private information without permission, and any other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and will take fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate.

Report abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior to the maintainers at oss@laserdata.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the privacy and security of the reporter will be respected.
